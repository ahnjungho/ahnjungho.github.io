About

My name is Ahn Jungho. I live in Suwon, South Korea. I studied Electical Engineering and Philosophy at Seoul National University, but I live a life that is irrelevant to my major. I now work as a programmer.

Usually I spend most of the time on programming. I develop web using Python and Django. I have interests in web front-end technology. Sometimes I make web sites using Wordpress by request and create simple iOS applications for my needs.

I'm a western classical music enthusiast, with Bach being my favorite composer. I love small orchestral works composed during the Baroque and Classical period. I play classical guitar within the limits of what I learned at university. I'm not good at playing guitar but I enjoy it very much.
